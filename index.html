Who Migrates to and from NYC?

These charts show the number of people who moved into or left NYC during particular time periods, according to census survey data. While NYC has seen wide variations in migration rates through the decades, certain groups are especially inclined to come or go.

Hover over the bars to see trendlines and details for each grouping.
